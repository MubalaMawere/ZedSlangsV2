About Zed Slangs Dictionary

Celebrating Zambian culture through language and community

Our Mission

Zed Slangs Dictionary is a cultural preservation project that celebrates the rich linguistic heritage of Zambia. We aim to document, share, and educate people about common Zambian slangs from multiple languages: Bemba, Nyanja, and Tonga.

Language is more than just communication‚Äîit's a window into culture, history, and identity. Through this platform, we hope to bridge generational gaps and preserve the vibrant expressions that make Zambian culture unique.

Cultural Importance

Zambia is home to over 70 ethnic groups, each with their own linguistic traditions. Slangs evolve as languages adapt to modern life, reflecting contemporary Zambian society and the fusion of traditional and urban cultures.

By documenting these slangs, we're not just preserving words‚Äîwe're preserving stories, humor, and the spirit of Zambian communities. Each slang carries with it cultural context, social dynamics, and historical significance.
